What you can hope to gain is insight I have picked up from bootstrapped frameworks, techniques and theory from various walks of life such as chemistry, software engineering, AND X and applied it to the field of marketing.

I will talk about a framework and philosophy coined Growth Marketing, a technique which differs from traditional undisciplined and low impact marketing. Growth marketing focuses on clearly measurable and directly impactful or profitable marketing initiatives.
