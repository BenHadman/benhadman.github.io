Automate The Boring Shit

Posted on 1 January 2018 1 January 2018

It would be interesting to hear how this works in other companies... let me know what your comapny does in the comments below.

**The problem**

We're a growing company with many products and probably half the size marketing team we need to be able to support such a large range of products. Each person should be adding value to the company with everything they do There simply isn't enough resource or time for repetitive, administrative tasks to take up our time. I'm a huge fan of ATBS (Automate the Boring Shit) and a big advocate of a principle one of my university lecturers taught me, > an hour in the library can save a day in the lab This blog will likely feature a lot of automation...

**The scenario**

We get a ton of enquiries from various sources; forms, direct email, sales prospecting, ... Most of our leads however come fro the web through form submissions. These submissions are routed into a central email inbox and are then entered manually by a human being into our CRM system (Salesforce). The human then has to look up whose sales territory that lead belongs to and forward the submission details via email to the salesforce.

**The solution**

ZAPIER is a hugely powerful tool for marketers to link up the ever increasing ecosytem of software tools we use. In essence it is a visual builder for APIs that allows you to join systems together without having to touch any code. You build out 'zaps' which are workflows based on a trigger which invokes actions which are operators on the input data. *IMAGE OF THE ZAPIER HERE* Using this I was able to take the form inputs and put them straight into manual process of lead entry
